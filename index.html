About

Haluan is the competitive hip-hop dance team housed under the Filipino Student Association at the University of California, Santa Cruz. Haluan, which translates to "mixed" in Tagalog, was founded in 1992 by a group that wished to perform dance at the Pilipino Culture Celebration, an annual showcase put on by FSA at UCSC. Since then, Haluan has grown to become the only competitive hip-hop dance team based in Santa Cruz competing at events such as Prelude, Collaboration NorCal, Bridge NorCal and Breakthrough.
